Instructions

The premise is simple: You navigate your character (the red colored person) to successfully make it to the front of a line using the arrow keys.

You start on the right hand side and move to the front of the line on the left. After choosing your line push the left key to enter the queue.

You must continue to press the left arrow key to move up in the line whe a spot opens up. If you wait too long the customers behind you will take your spot.

If at any time you wish to change lines simply use the up or down arrow keys to jump between lines.
